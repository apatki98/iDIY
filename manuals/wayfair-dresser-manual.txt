WAYFAIR MERCURY ROW KAVIA 6-DRAWER DRESSER
Model: W004924293
Assembly Instructions

=========================================
IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
=========================================
- This product requires assembly by two adults.
- Assembly time: approximately 60-90 minutes.
- Do not use power tools unless specified.
- Secure dresser to wall using included anti-tip kit to prevent tipping.
- Keep all hardware and small parts away from children during assembly.

=========================================
PARTS LIST
=========================================
A - Left Side Panel (x1)
B - Right Side Panel (x1)
C - Top Panel (x1)
D - Bottom Panel (x1)
E - Back Panel (x1) — thin MDF board
F - Center Divider (x1) — vertical support
G - Drawer Box Small (x3) — for top row
H - Drawer Box Large (x3) — for bottom row
I - Drawer Front Small (x3)
J - Drawer Front Large (x3)
K - Drawer Slide Left (x6 pairs)
L - Drawer Slide Right (x6 pairs)

=========================================
HARDWARE LIST
=========================================
H1 - Cam Lock Bolt (x24) — metal bolt, threads into panels
H2 - Cam Lock Nut (x24) — round disc, locks into bolt
H3 - Wood Dowel (x16) — small wooden peg for alignment
H4 - Phillips Head Screw 30mm (x20) — for slides and back panel
H5 - Phillips Head Screw 15mm (x12) — for drawer fronts
H6 - Hex Bolt M6 (x4) — for center divider
H7 - Allen Key (x1) — included, for hex bolts
H8 - Anti-Tip Bracket (x1) — wall anchor kit
H9 - Wall Screws (x2) — for anti-tip bracket
H10 - Drawer Handle (x6) — brushed nickel

=========================================
TOOLS REQUIRED
=========================================
- Phillips Head Screwdriver #2 (REQUIRED)
- Rubber Mallet or Hammer with cloth (REQUIRED)
- Level (RECOMMENDED)
- Tape Measure (RECOMMENDED)
- Drill with Phillips bit (OPTIONAL — speeds up screw driving)
- Pencil (for marking wall anchor position)

=========================================
STEP 1: UNBOX AND ORGANIZE (15 minutes)
=========================================
1. Carefully open the box and remove all panels. Lay them on a soft surface to prevent scratching.
2. Sort all hardware into groups using the hardware list above.
3. Verify you have all parts. Contact Wayfair support if anything is missing.
4. Identify each panel by its label sticker (located on the back or underside).
TIP: Lay panels face-down on cardboard from the box to protect the finish.

=========================================
STEP 2: ATTACH DRAWER SLIDES TO SIDE PANELS (10 minutes)
=========================================
1. Take Left Side Panel (A) and lay it inner-face-up.
2. Locate the pre-drilled holes for drawer slides — 3 rows of 2 holes on each side.
3. Attach Drawer Slide Left (K) to each row using Phillips Head Screws 30mm (H4). Each slide uses 2 screws.
4. Repeat for Right Side Panel (B) using Drawer Slide Right (L).
IMPORTANT: Slides have a front and back — the release tab faces the front of the dresser.
NOTE: Top row slides are spaced closer together (for small drawers). Bottom row slides are wider apart (for large drawers).

=========================================
STEP 3: ASSEMBLE THE MAIN FRAME (20 minutes, TWO PEOPLE RECOMMENDED)
=========================================
1. Insert Wood Dowels (H3) into the pre-drilled holes on the top edge of Left Side Panel (A) and Right Side Panel (B). Tap gently with mallet. Use 2 dowels per connection point.
2. Insert Cam Lock Bolts (H1) into the pre-drilled holes on Bottom Panel (D). Twist clockwise until snug.
3. Stand Left Side Panel (A) upright. Align Bottom Panel (D) so the cam lock bolts insert into the cam lock nuts (H2) pre-installed in the side panel. Turn each cam lock nut clockwise with a Phillips screwdriver to lock.
4. Repeat to attach Right Side Panel (B) to the other end of Bottom Panel (D).
5. Place Top Panel (C) on top. Align dowels and cam lock connections. Lock all cam lock nuts.
6. The frame should now be a rectangular box (open front, open back).
IMPORTANT: Have a second person hold panels while you secure the cam locks. The frame is unstable until all connections are locked.

=========================================
STEP 4: INSTALL CENTER DIVIDER (5 minutes)
=========================================
1. Slide Center Divider (F) into the notch in Bottom Panel (D).
2. Align the top of the divider with the pre-drilled holes in Top Panel (C).
3. Secure with Hex Bolts M6 (H6) — 2 at the top, 2 at the bottom. Use the included Allen Key (H7).
4. The divider splits the dresser into left and right columns of 3 drawers each.

=========================================
STEP 5: ATTACH BACK PANEL (10 minutes)
=========================================
1. Carefully flip the frame face-down. (Two people recommended.)
2. Place Back Panel (E) — the thin MDF board — over the back of the frame.
3. Align edges flush with the frame.
4. Secure with Phillips Head Screws 30mm (H4) every 6 inches around the perimeter. Use approximately 12 screws.
IMPORTANT: The back panel provides structural rigidity. Do not skip screws or leave gaps.
TIP: Start with the four corners, then fill in the remaining screws.

=========================================
STEP 6: INSERT DRAWERS (15 minutes)
=========================================
1. Stand the dresser upright in its final position.
2. For each drawer:
   a. Pull the drawer slide rail out from the dresser frame until it clicks and locks extended.
   b. Align the drawer box side rails with the extended frame rails.
   c. Slide the drawer in firmly until you hear a click.
   d. Test the drawer — it should slide smoothly and close fully.
3. Install Small Drawer Boxes (G) in the top 3 slots (above center divider height).
4. Install Large Drawer Boxes (H) in the bottom 3 slots.
NOTE: If a drawer doesn't slide smoothly, check that the slide rails are properly aligned and screwed in straight.

=========================================
STEP 7: ATTACH DRAWER FRONTS (10 minutes)
=========================================
1. For each Small Drawer Front (I):
   a. Align the front with the drawer box, ensuring even gaps on all sides.
   b. Secure from inside the drawer box using Phillips Head Screws 15mm (H5) — 2 screws per front.
2. For each Large Drawer Front (J):
   a. Same process — align and secure with 2 screws each.
3. Attach Drawer Handles (H10) to each front using the pre-drilled holes and included bolts (from inside the drawer).
TIP: Use tape to temporarily hold the drawer front in position while you drive the screws.

=========================================
STEP 8: SECURE TO WALL — ANTI-TIP KIT (5 minutes, REQUIRED)
=========================================
WARNING: Unsecured furniture can tip over and cause serious injury or death, especially to children.
1. Position the dresser in its final location against the wall.
2. Attach Anti-Tip Bracket (H8) to the back of the dresser top using the pre-drilled holes.
3. Mark the wall where the bracket meets it. Use a pencil.
4. Drill pilot holes into the wall at the marked positions. Use wall anchors if not drilling into a stud.
5. Secure the bracket to the wall using Wall Screws (H9).
6. Test by gently pulling the dresser forward — it should not tip.
THIS STEP IS MANDATORY. DO NOT SKIP.

=========================================
STEP 9: FINAL CHECKS (5 minutes)
=========================================
1. Open and close all 6 drawers. Each should glide smoothly.
2. Check that the dresser is level using a Level tool. Adjust position or use furniture pads if needed.
3. Verify the anti-tip bracket is secure.
4. Tighten any cam lock nuts that feel loose.
5. Dispose of packaging materials responsibly.
6. Enjoy your new dresser!

=========================================
CARE INSTRUCTIONS
=========================================
- Wipe with a soft, damp cloth. Do not use abrasive cleaners.
- Avoid placing hot items directly on the surface.
- Do not exceed maximum drawer weight: 15 lbs per small drawer, 25 lbs per large drawer.
- Periodically check all hardware connections and tighten if needed.
